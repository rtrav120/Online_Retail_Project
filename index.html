Who We Are

We come from a group of online sellers that have regrouped and considered their own new model.

After spending time in the online retail world and seeing some of the challenges faced we set out to make things easier.

The Why

The online retail market are full of buyers and sellers.
Some are trying to look for quick deals why others are trying to make a quick buck.

Its often hard to find items for your stores and more time consuming than profitable.

Our goal is to eliminate that time spent and to create lower prices using our system.

How We Operate

  1. Items will be found using resources such as automation and virtual outsourcing.
  2. The elimination of platform fees will also further savings for our customers.
  3. By utilizing drop shipping, we will be able to add further discounts on our items for customers who accept offers early.

Who You Might Be

Whether your a reseller or a retail customer our products can be bought by you.

Resellers and retail customers can simply order from us and have it shiped directly to themselves
Resellers can place orders on our items and have the option to ship directly to their purchasers if they've lined one up.
Resellers who set up with us can also have the option of prepurchasing up to 5 items of one type.
