The Why

The online retail market are full of buyers and sellers.
Some are trying to look for quick deals why others are trying to make a quick buck.

People trying to make some money by selling their items are often harrased to lower their price or are sent offers way below the typical value.
This is because resellers selling on a platform often have to find a really good deal for an item just to turn the wheel on their bottom line.

Our goal is to allow the interests of the sellers and the resellers to intersect by scanning the markets and cutting out the middle man.

How We Operate

Using our resources, we scan the market looking for deals so you don't have to.
By scanning broadly for deals and cutting out platform fees we can sell you our items at prices lower than the average price.
Our customers who order our products earlier can enjoy another discount on the items by having us ship our purchased items directly to them.

Who You Might Be

Whether your a reseller or a retail customer our products can be bought by you.

Resellers and retail customers can simply order from us and have it shiped directly to themselves
Resellers can place orders on our items and have the option to ship directly to their purchasers if they've lined one up.
Resellers who set up with us can also have the option of prepurchasing up to 5 items of one type.
